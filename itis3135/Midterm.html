I Welcome You To My Hobby Site!!

About me

My name is Joshua Ogundele and my hobby is cooking various healthy dishes. I was born and raised in Raleigh, North Carolina and I am currently 21 years old. I attended Wake Technical Community College for two years for financial and progressive benefit purposes. After my two years was accomplished, I decided to move to Charlotte in the Fall of 2022 to attend UNC Charlotte as a junior classified transfer student to continuously pursue my Bachelors degree in Computer Science.

When I Started

I started to cook healthy dishes regualrly in the year of 2020 during quarantine. During the pandemic it allowed me to stay home and conduct more research about many things and I took that opportunity seriously to see what types of healthy foods I could be consuming for health beneficial purposes. This is when I tried quinoa for the first time; which eventually became my favorite grain dish to implement in my diet.

Where I do my Hobby

I usually enjoy cooking the most in my own home, however, I would not mind cooking anywhere else especially if food is needed during a particualar moment outside my home along with the right food ingredients provided for me to cook with. One thing I will admit is the fact that I do cook my best when I am not watched and/or observed by another individual because there is less pressure put on my shoulders to cook with a great outcome. Cooking in my own home just gives me a different kind of enjoyment experience! I love playing music while cooking as well because it brings out the enthusiasm within my personality.

How I do my hobby

I start off with a warmup which consists of me playing some of the basic riffs I know. Then I start playing around with different chords and improvising a bit. This is where most of the fun comes from. When you start improvising and adding your own style to songs you know, you can end up making completely different songs and parodies.

Why

I consider myself as an individual who strongly desires to achieve my fullest potential in all aspects of life; hence, I believe cooking helathy dishes progesses me towards that goal mentally, emotionally, physically, and other important aspects of my life since what one consumes daily has an essentail impact on one's overall well-being. Thus, this is my motivation to cook healthy dishes on a regualr basis.
