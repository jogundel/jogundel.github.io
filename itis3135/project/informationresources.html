Information Resources is highly essential regarding the process of getting your taxes filed. The purpose of information resources is to provide to each client the specified qualifications and/or requried documentation for the process of one's taxes to be filed in a correct and proper manner. This will ensure great organization and prevention from any major mistakes while filing one's taxes.

To proceed with every tax preparation process all of the following is required per client:
  • Social Security information
  • Drivers License information
  • W2, 1099, Profit and Law Statement (P/L), K1, 1099R, and any other tax related documentation information within the possession of the client to ensure successful tax preparation.
