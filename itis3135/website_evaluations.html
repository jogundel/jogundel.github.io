Good Website Example

  • C: From my point of view in a contrast perspective, I believe this website is a good example because the colors used on this website is not too bright or dull. The colors used make it easy for the audience to read all the information given within the website.
  • R: From my point of view in a repititional perspective, I believe this website is a good example because it does not repeat any unnecessary information. Instead, the consistency is great for the conveinence of the audience to keep up with all detailed information within the NBA.
  • A: From my point of view, from an alignment perspective, I believe the alignment is great! Everything is easy to navigate through and nothing seems to be out of place.
  • P: From my point of view in a proximity perspective, everything seems to be consistent in terms of color and shape of the objects used within the website. Hence, it is an exceptional example in this regard.
